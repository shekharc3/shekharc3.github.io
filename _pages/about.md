My name is Chandra Shekhar, and I am an Assistant Professor of Environmental Ethics in the Department of Geography and Environmental Sustainability at the University of Oklahoma. I completed a PhD in Public Policy and Environmental Philosophy at MIT, followed by a postdoctoral position at the London School of Economics. My research interests lie in the political economy of governance, environmental ethics, and social theory.
